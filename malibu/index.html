Project Malibu

I've be interested in building a quadcopter for a while, mostly as an exercise in learning basic electronics and aerodynamics. This page will hopefully chronicle the drone's construction and deployment.

There are a few basic goals for the project:

  • Basic flight within eye sight with pre-built controller
  • FPV video streaming from the drone to MacBook
  • SDR radio control of the drone from MacBook with arbitrary controller (I'm thinking wheel)
  • Autonomous flight of some kind
  • Real-time video analysis and feedback (object recognition)
